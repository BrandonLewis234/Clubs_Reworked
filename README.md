<h1><a href="https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/clubs-and-flails" target="_blank" style="color: #ffffff; text-decoration: none">Clubs and Flails</a></h1><h2 style="color: #eccafa; font-weight: bold;">Introduces clubs and flails as new weapon types usable by the player.</h2>

<span style="color: #fff;">Previously, clubs and rusty flails only were usable by mobs, and spawning them in creative mode had limited usage—no blocking and no signature move.</span>

<span style="color: #fff;">This mod aims to change that, while also introducing the <b style="color: #eccafa;">full progression line of flails</b>.</span>

***

## **<span style="color: #fff;">Overview</span>**

<span style="color: #fff;">This mod introduces two new weapons; <b style="color: #eccafa;">clubs</b> and <b style="color: #eccafa;">flails</b>. They are new</span> <b style="color: #eccafa;">one-handed melee weapons</b> <span style="color: #fff;">with:</span>

*   <span style="color: #fff;">A unique attack pattern</span>
*   <span style="color: #fff;">Charged attack</span>
*   <span style="color: #fff;">Signature move</span>

<h4 style="color: #eccafa;">The crude club and flail are craftable without a workbench.</h4>

<span style="color: #fff;">The other clubs and flails are craftable in the blacksmith's bench─found under the "maces" category─with tier requirements following with their rarity.</span>
